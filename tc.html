About Us

Welcome to Destinations Unlimited

A premier travel company dedicated to redefining the standards of Outbound and Inbound Tourism since our establishment in January 2009.

Driven by a passion for crafting unforgettable travel experiences, Destinations Unlimited has meticulously curated over 75 tours, tailored to meet the unique preferences of our discerning clientele. Our commitment to excellence is evident in every aspect of our tours, which embody the epitome of luxury and quality, showcasing the rich tapestry of local nature, art, culture, social fabric, and cuisine.

Our Vision

Our focus lies in promoting outbound group and individual travel experiences abroad, while also successfully facilitating inbound tours that introduce visitors to the enchanting landscapes and vibrant culture of India. Over the past decade, we have proudly welcomed numerous groups and families to explore the diverse beauty of our country, ensuring memorable experiences that linger in their hearts for years to come.

At Destinations Unlimited, we believe that every journey should be as unique as the traveler embarking upon it. With our unparalleled expertise and personalized approach, we strive to transform your travel dreams into reality, one unforgettable adventure at a time.

Destinations Unlimited is the brainchild of Kausalya Padmanabhan, a seasoned professional with over four decades of experience in the travel industry. With an unwavering dedication to meeting travelers' needs and exceeding their expectations, Kausalya embarked on this journey in 2009. Her extensive tenure in the travel sector spans across various domains, including domestic and international tours, both inbound and outbound.
